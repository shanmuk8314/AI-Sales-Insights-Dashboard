# Problem Statement

Mediwave Life Sciences handles sales data that is often maintained and analyzed through spreadsheets and CSV files. As sales records grow, manually reviewing data to identify top-performing products, declining products, and weak sales regions becomes time-consuming and inefficient. Manual analysis can also lead to errors, delayed reporting, and missed business opportunities.

The company requires a solution that can automatically process sales data, generate meaningful insights, and present the information in an easy-to-understand format. The proposed AI Sales Insights Dashboard aims to automate sales analysis, reduce manual effort, and support faster data-driven decision-making.

# Abstract

The AI Sales Insights Dashboard is a web-based application designed to simplify sales performance analysis for Mediwave Life Sciences. The system allows administrators to upload sales data through CSV files, which are then processed and analyzed to identify top-performing products, declining products, sales trends, and weak sales regions.

The solution is developed using React.js for the frontend, Node.js and Express.js for the backend, and MongoDB for data storage. By combining data processing, analytics, and visualization, the dashboard provides actionable business insights through an interactive interface. The project aims to improve reporting efficiency, reduce manual effort, and support informed business decisions.
